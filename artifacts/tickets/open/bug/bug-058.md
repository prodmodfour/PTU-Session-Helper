## Summary

PTU distinguishes "HP loss" (e.g., Belly Drum, Life Orb recoil) from "damage" (attack-based). HP loss should bypass defense calculations and injury checks. Currently all HP reduction goes through the damage pathway, which incorrectly triggers massive damage injury checks for effects like Belly Drum (50% HP loss).

## Suggested Fix

Add a `hpLoss` flag or separate endpoint that reduces HP without triggering injury/defense calculations.

## Impact

Belly Drum and Life Orb users receive incorrect injury penalties. Cross-domain overlap with healing audit finding R012/R035 (Pain Split/Endeavor).

## Resolution Log
