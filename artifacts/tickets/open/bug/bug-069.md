## Summary

Per decree-054, the `HpReductionType` union must be expanded to distinguish "recoil" hpLoss (Life Orb, which absorbs temp HP) from "self-cost" hpLoss (Belly Drum, which bypasses temp HP).

## Required Implementation

1. **Expand `HpReductionType`** in `combatant.service.ts`: split `'hpLoss'` into `'recoil'` and `'selfCost'` (or add a sub-type field). `'setHp'` remains unchanged.

2. **Update `calculateDamage()` temp HP absorption** (line 118): change the condition from `lossType === 'damage'` to absorb temp HP for both `'damage'` and `'recoil'`, while bypassing for `'selfCost'` and `'setHp'`.

3. **Update callers**: find all places that pass `'hpLoss'` as a lossType and classify each as `'recoil'` or `'selfCost'`:
   - Life Orb recoil -> `'recoil'`
   - Belly Drum -> `'selfCost'`
   - Other "loses HP" effects -> classify case-by-case

4. **Preserve existing behavior**: massive damage and heavily-injured exemptions should apply to both `'recoil'` and `'selfCost'` (same as current `'hpLoss'` behavior).

## Notes

- decree-054 establishes the precedent: intent determines temp HP interaction. "Incoming" costs absorb temp HP; "deliberate sacrifice" costs bypass it.
- decree-004 (massive damage uses real HP after temp HP absorption) remains valid and applies to the new recoil type.
