## Summary

GmToastContainer.vue (now in the GM layout) has no `onUnmounted` lifecycle hook to clear pending toast timers. When navigating from any GM page to a non-GM page, the module-scoped `timers` Map and `toasts` ref survive indefinitely, leaking timers.

## Required Fix

Add `onUnmounted(() => dismissAll())` to GmToastContainer.vue. Destructure `dismissAll` from `useGmToast()`.

## Impact

Medium — timer leak accumulates on navigation. Moving GmToastContainer to the GM layout reduces frequency (layout persists across GM routes) but doesn't eliminate the issue.
