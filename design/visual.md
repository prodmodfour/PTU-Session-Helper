# Sprites
- Gen 5 and below: Pokemon Black 2/White 2 sprites via PokeAPI
- Gen 6 and above: Latest 3D game sprites via PokeAPI
- Shiny variants supported

# Theme (Pokemon Scarlet/Violet Dark)
- **Background**: Deep navy-black (#0f0f1a)
- **Accent**: Scarlet red (#e63946) with violet (#7c3aed) accents
- **Text**: Warm white (#f0f0f5)
- **Effects**: Glass-morphism, gradient borders, glow shadows

# 4K Optimization
- Group View scales up for 4K displays (3000px+ breakpoint)
- Larger sprites (240px), fonts (1.5x), and spacing
- Enhanced glow effects for visibility on TV

# Health Bars
- B2W2-style gradient health bars
- Color states: Green (healthy), Yellow (warning), Red (critical)
- Pulsing animation on critical HP
