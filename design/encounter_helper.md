# Sides
- There are three "Sides"
- Players, Allies, Enemies
- Allies sometimes not applicable

# Automation
- Automatically sorts initiative based on speed + initiative bonuses
- Handles trainer vs full contact battle differences, clear ui differences between the two
- Automatically handles xp, level ups
- Automatically handles initiative change after all actions used by a character or pass turn pressed (or ready action chosen (requires typing text for ready action))
- Automatically handles move damage and keeps log of moves used and targets used on + effects
- Move damage is Set Damage and not rolled damage
